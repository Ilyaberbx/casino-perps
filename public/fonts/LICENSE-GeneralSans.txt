General Sans
============

Designer:  Indian Type Foundry (ITF)
Source:    Fontshare — https://www.fontshare.com/fonts/general-sans
File:      GeneralSans-Variable.woff2 (variable, weight 200–700)

License:   ITF Free Font License (FFL)
           https://www.fontshare.com/licenses/itf-ffl

Used here as a free, commercially-usable substitute for Aeonik (Cotype
Foundry). General Sans is the closest open alternative in the geometric-
grotesque genre. See docs/adr/0073-aeonik-substitute-single-font.md.

Summary of the ITF Free Font License (the linked license is authoritative):
  - Free for personal AND commercial use.
  - Embedding in documents, applications, and websites is permitted, including
    self-hosting the webfont files (as done here under public/fonts/).
  - The font software itself may not be sold or sub-licensed on its own.
  - No warranty is provided.

If a genuine, licensed Aeonik webfont is procured later, drop the Aeonik
woff2 into this folder and repoint the `--font-sans` @font-face / family in
apps/client/src/app/index.css. The swap is a single-file change.
